Our Mission

Semper Fit Training is a combination of various routines and exercises designed by a US Marine and National Bodybuilder to challenge all of our clientele. This is not just for elite athletes, but designed for everyone no matter your level of fitness, beginner or advanced. Semper Fit will not only get you in the best shape of your life but also make you the leanest you ever been. Our training style is designed to shed lots of excess body fat and build lean muscle. We also train you in the proper bio mechanics of each exercise and how to design an effective workout. Along the way you will be provided with extensive nutritional support to help reach your goals. Semper Fit is for both men and women all ages with experience from 15 to 93. If changing your body, getting into great physical shape, or just feeling better Semper Fit is the right choice for you.
