One-on-one

One-on-one training consists of guidance provided either in your own home or at a personal studio in your area. Thinking that you absolutely must go to a gym to have an effective workout is a common mistake that I want to help you avoid. Although many larger chain gyms may have more of the equipment that can help to maximize your workout, I can show you how to fully realize your fitness potential in an environment that is more comforting to you, whether it be a small studio down the street from you or your own living room.

Group

I enjoy working with groups just as much as I do single individuals. Groups that I train range from 2 to 4 clients at a time and are trained in various areas. This is often a more affordable way to break into the fitness world not only because group rates do differ from individual rates, but also because familiar company takes away much of the intimidation of the gym while inspiring you to give that bit of extra effort.
If you have ten or more people, I will travel to your location and host a bootcamp style workout. This is often ideal for corporate group as well as sporting group events. These group bootcamps usually consist of 1-hour sessions and can be held at a local park, school, or even your workplace parking lot!

My rehabilitation-geared training will help you get back on your feet without missing a step along the way.
